    Point Processes

    In statistics and probability theory, a point process or point field is a collection of mathematical points randomly located on some underlying mathematical space such as the real line, the Cartesian plane, or more abstract spaces.

    Ensemble / Hybrid Machine Learning Models

    In applied mathematics, statistics and machine learning, hybrid and ensemble learning techniques are used to combine the predictions of multiple classifiers to produce a single classifier. Ensembles aim to reduce the generalisation error and have notably better results when there is a significant diversity among the models.
